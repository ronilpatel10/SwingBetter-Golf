Golf Training Plans

Use these videos and quick tips to practice different parts of your golf game.

Driver
Training

Work on distance, balance, and hitting straighter tee shots.

Quick Tips

Keep your feet shoulder-width apart, swing smooth, and focus on solid contact instead of swinging too hard.

Iron
Training

Improve accuracy, ball contact, and control with your irons.

Quick Tips

Hit down on the ball, keep your head steady, and finish balanced after every swing.

Putting
Practice

Build better distance control and improve short putts.

Quick Tips

Keep your stroke smooth, watch your alignment, and practice short putts from 3 to 6 feet.
